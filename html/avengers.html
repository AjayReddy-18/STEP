Information

Ultron is a supervillian appearing in American comic books published by Marvel comics. He is a highly intelligent self-aware artificial intelligence is a robot body who develops a god complex and a grudge against his creator Hank Pym, with whom he would later merge. His initial goal is to destroy humanity in an attempt at creating world peace has brought him into conflitct with the Avengers.

Jocsta, a robot created by Ultron featured in a number of Ultron storylines, was created by Jim Shooter and George Perez for The Avengers
